# Physics Scope

## Principles
- SI units internally
- Equations must be physically valid and documented
- No fake physics for visuals

## Milikan (flagship)
- Forces: $F_g$, buoyancy, drag (Stokes), electric force
- Terminal velocities (falling/rising)
- Radius estimation from falling speed
- Charge computation and Cunningham correction
